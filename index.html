01 // About us

Welcome to Nirman 4.0: Where Innovation Meets Transformation

Register Now

Nirman 3rd Edition, hosted by Silicon Innovation and Promotion Cell, is an annual inter-college tech fest that unites over 1000 tech enthusiasts from across the nation. As the third iteration of this vibrant event, Nirman 3rd Edition is set to be a weekend filled with cutting-edge technology, innovation, and collaboration. Whether you’re an experienced coder or just starting out, Nirman welcomes students from all technical and management backgrounds to participate in a hybrid experience that emphasizes learning by doing. Through workshops, hackathons, and tech challenges, participants will have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience, grow their skillsets, and connect with fellow innovators. By supporting Nirman 3rd Edition, you help us provide resources, rewards, meals, and an unforgettable experience for our participants, empowering the next generation of thinkers and creators. Join us on this journey to make technology accessible, exciting, and transformative.

06 // Faq

FAQs: Your Queries,
Our Insights

Explore the answers that empower your experience, guiding you through every aspect of our event. Together, we'll uncover insights to enhance your journey and engagement.

I am a beginner, can I participate ?
You should register for Nirman because this event is all about getting that experience! The events provide a great environment to get started.
Can I participate in all of these events ?
You are welcome to participate in any or all of these events. If you want to participate in only one, you are able to do so. These events include people with a variety of skill levels and abilities. Teams of 3-4 are required.
What is a hackathon ?
Hackathons allow students to come together to create tech projects in a collaborative environment. Throughout the event, teams of students will brainstorm, design, code, and create amazing projects.
Is Nirman an offline or online event ?
This will be an offline/In-person event, held on the campus of Silicon Institute of Technology, Bhubaneswar.
Can anyone outside Silicon Institute of Technology participate in nirman ?
Everyone from any institute in India can participate in this event.
What is the registration fee ?
The registration fees for Hacknation is ₹100 per member and for rest of the events ₹50 per member.
Where can I see Event details?
Kindly visit the events page and click on the view details option.
